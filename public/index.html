Who I am

A technology enthusiast who hails from Houston, and has never stopped looking to the stars above. A consumate problem solver that wants to always know more, so I can solve the problems that will arise.

I solve Problems

I am a problem solver who loves to find the problems that plague us in day to day life, and seek out to solve those problems to become more efficient. Some problems have simple solutions, some require complex solutions, that may cause their own problems, but I have a drive to find them and conquer them.

Professionally, I have been a problem solver with a client-centric focus. With 10 years of Enterprise IT and a Computer Science background, I apply my problem-solving acumen to the real world.

I am Simply Me

I am part of a growing world of culture, art, technology, and ideas. Wanting to experience the fusion of all of these facets drives me to become better than I could possibly imagine. In a reciprical nature, I hope the World is a better place with my experience and ideas.
